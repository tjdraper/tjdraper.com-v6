> One is focused on the new [EarPod headphones](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9-FmipwLsHc), while another shows off the [new Panorama feature](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xtm4ySJQPOc) in iOS 6, and a third touts the [larger screen](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V4IRsCjMqdI) of the iPhone 5. The fourth ad shows how the iPhone 5 is the [perfect size for "your thumb"](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A1Rc4MDmr8o), a subtle jab at the larger screens of some Android phones that can be difficult to use with one hand.

Now _these_ are good ads. I don't know what went wrong and where it went wrong with the last batch of Mac ads, but these are great.
