> **NoMoreiTunes**
> **Safari Extension**
> Preventing iTunes Preview and App Store pages in Safari from launching iTunes since 2010.

I love this extension. Apple has great preview support in Safari. I want to preview it in Safari before I load iTunes. Sometimes I click on a link and don’t realize it’s going to launch in Safari, finish loading, then load in iTunes. This fixes that.
