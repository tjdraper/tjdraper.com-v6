Long story short, the least I can pay for two smartphones with a 1GB pool of data (not enough) is $130. I really need the 2GB plan so I’m actually looking at $140. I hate AT&T with a fiery burning passion, but they are still the least expensive option for me right now at $110 a month. Their service is probably the most awful on the planet, but it gets me by and I can’t pay $140 a month right now for Verizon. This is just stupid. If Verizon cut these prices almost (not quite, perhaps) in half, I would consider them quite fair.
